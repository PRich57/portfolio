About Me

Hey, I'm Pete Richards and I'm a Student in University of Denver's Coding Boot Camp!

Back in May 2023, I made the decision to exit sales industry and begin pursuing a career in a field I've been dreaming about for several years: Software Development. Since making this decision in May, I've completed Harvard CS50 on edX.org, signed up for this boot camp, and began working through The Odin Project while waiting for my classes to begin. I've known for years this industry could truly become a passion of mine, but from all the knowledge I've gained over the past three months the one piece of information that's clearer than any other is this: I love everything about coding, creating, collaborating, researching, and problem solving. Whether it be Software or Web Development, I've found my calling!
